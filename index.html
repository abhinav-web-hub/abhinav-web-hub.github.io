About Pesticides Chemical India (PCI)

Pesticides Chemical India (PCI) is a premier chemical manufacturing and formulating enterprise headquartered in Hapur, Uttar Pradesh. We specialize in producing top-tier crop protection solutions designed to maximize yield and protect agricultural produce from pest infestations and diseases.

Driven by scientific precision and stringent quality control, our extensive product portfolio includes high-potency Insecticides, Fungicides, and customized agrochemical formulations trusted by farmers, distributors, and agricultural enterprises.
